About Me

From Algorithms
to Aesthetics

I come from a computer science background, and I still really enjoy it. I like understanding how things work under the hood, and turning ideas into real, usable products.

At the same time, I’m drawn to design in digital and virtual spaces. UX and UI are a big part of that, but I also love visual design overall - layout, typography, color, and interaction.

I don’t see CS and design as separate paths. My technical background helps me design things that make sense, are realistic to build, and work well with engineering teams, while still looking and feeling polished.
